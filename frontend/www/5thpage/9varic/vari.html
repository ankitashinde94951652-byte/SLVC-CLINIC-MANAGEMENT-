What Are Varicose Veins?

Varicose veins are enlarged, twisted veins that usually appear on the legs due to faulty valves and poor blood circulation. They may cause pain, swelling, heaviness, itching, and skin discoloration.

Why Varicose Veins Occur

  • Prolonged standing or sitting
  • Genetic predisposition
  • Pregnancy and hormonal changes
  • Obesity and sedentary lifestyle
