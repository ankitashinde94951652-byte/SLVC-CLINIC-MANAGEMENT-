Gangrene/ blackening of toes

Understanding Gangrene: Causes, Symptoms, and Treatment Options

Benefits of Early Detection

Early detection allows timely intervention, helping prevent infection spread, reduce complications, and improve recovery outcomes.

Treatment for Gangrene / Blackening of Toes

  • Surgical Intervention: Removal of dead tissue or amputation if required
  • Antibiotics: Control and prevent infection spread
  • Revascularization: Angioplasty or bypass surgery to restore blood flow
  • Pain Management: Medications to manage discomfort
